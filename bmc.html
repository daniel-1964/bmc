bmc manual page¶

Synopsis¶

bmc [options] <source-file | ->

Description¶

BMC parses braille music code and converts it to digital music notation formats suitable for exchange with users of typical visual music notation.

Options¶

--lilypond Convert to LilyPond music engraver format.
--musicxml Convert to MusicXML.

See also¶
